## About the Company

**Q: What is Perfect Day?**
A: Perfect Day is an AI-driven event planning and deal discovery platform. It helps users organize any type of celebration or event — birthdays, weddings, corporate gatherings, or casual outings — while connecting them with personalized deals from local and major businesses. The company is based in California, USA.

**Q: What problem does Perfect Day solve?**
A: Event planning is typically fragmented across multiple tools and platforms, causing inefficiency and stress. Perfect Day consolidates the entire process into one app — planning, vendor discovery, scheduling, budgeting, and deal access — in a single, personalized experience.

**Q: Is Perfect Day just for big events?**
A: No. Perfect Day is designed for any occasion, from complex multi-vendor weddings to a simple day out with friends. The one-click planning feature can generate a full itinerary for everyday activities just as easily as large events.

## Features & Product

**Q: What are the core features of the app?**
A: The main features include:
- Step-by-step event planning with guided checklists
- Personalized recommendations based on user preferences and past activity
- One-click AI itinerary generation based on location and preferences
- Integrated deals and exclusive offers from partner businesses
- Dynamic scheduling that recalibrates your plan when things change
- Social sharing and collaborative planning with friends or family
- Budget tracking and vendor coordination tools
- Guest list and attendee management

**Q: How does the one-click planning feature work?**
A: You tap the one-click option, the app reads your location and saved preferences, and generates a full day's itinerary automatically. You can then adjust any part of it before starting.

**Q: Can multiple people plan an event together?**
A: Yes. Perfect Day supports real-time collaboration, so planning committees, families, or teams can work on the same event simultaneously. Role-based access lets you delegate tasks while keeping sensitive details controlled.

**Q: Does the app integrate with Google or Apple Calendar?**
A: Calendar integration with Google and Apple Calendar is on the roadmap (Sprint 7.5) and is planned for a future release.

**Q: Is there an AR feature?**
A: Augmented reality for visualizing event setups in your actual space is planned. In focus group testing it received the highest rating (9.7/10) of any feature.

**Q: How does the app make personalized recommendations?**
A: The app uses AI and machine learning to analyze your preferences, past activity, and event type, then surfaces relevant vendors, activities, and offers. The system improves continuously as more data is collected.

## Deals & Business Partners

**Q: How do I access deals through the app?**
A: Deals and offers from partner businesses are integrated directly into your event plan. As you build your itinerary, relevant discounts and promotions appear automatically based on your preferences and location.

**Q: What kinds of businesses are on the platform?**
A: Perfect Day partners with a wide range — local venues, caterers, decorators, entertainment providers, and major brands. The vendor directory includes profiles, ratings, and reviews for each.

**Q: Can I get referral discounts?**
A: Yes. The app supports referral discounts and bonuses as part of its rewards system.

**Q: I found a better deal elsewhere. Can I still use it?**
A: The app does not restrict you to only its listed deals. However, exclusive partner offers are only accessible through the platform.

## Pricing & Plans

**Q: Is Perfect Day free?**
A: The app uses a freemium model. Core features are available for free. Premium features — advanced planning tools, enhanced personalization, analytics, and more — are available through a paid subscription.

**Q: Are there hidden fees?**
A: No. Pricing is transparent. Any costs for premium features or in-app transactions (like vendor bookings) are clearly displayed before you commit.

**Q: Does Perfect Day take a commission on bookings?**
A: Yes. A small percentage is charged on transactions made through the app, such as ticket purchases or vendor bookings.

## Privacy & Security

**Q: How is my personal data handled?**
A: Perfect Day uses encryption and undergoes regular security audits to protect user data. Privacy policies are clear and accessible, and users have control over how their data is used.

**Q: Does Perfect Day sell my data?**
A: No. User data is used internally to improve the platform and personalize recommendations. It is not sold to third parties.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes. All transactions go through secure, encrypted channels. Perfect Day does not store raw payment credentials.

## Account & Support

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Download the app, open it, and follow the sign-up flow. You will create a profile and set your preferences during onboarding.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Customer support is available 24/7 via in-app chat, email, and phone. There is also an in-app Help Center with FAQs, tutorials, and troubleshooting guides.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. You can request account deletion through the app settings or by contacting support. All personal data associated with the account will be removed per our privacy policy.

## For Businesses

**Q: How can my business join Perfect Day?**
A: Contact us through the business partnership inquiry form in the app or on our website. We partner with both local businesses and major brands.

**Q: What do businesses get from partnering with Perfect Day?**
A: Partner businesses gain access to a targeted user base, data-driven insights on customer preferences, visibility through the platform's recommendation engine, and a cost-effective marketing channel that reduces wasted spend.

**Q: How do I upload deals or promotions?**
A: Once onboarded as a partner, you will have access to a business dashboard where you can upload offers, track performance, and view analytics on how users interact with your listings.

**Q: Can I send last-minute availability notifications?**
A: Yes. The platform supports notifications from service providers about last-minute availability, which are pushed to relevant users in real time.

**Q: What analytics does the business dashboard provide?**
A: The dashboard shows engagement metrics, offer conversion data, and customer preference insights — enough to help you refine your offerings and targeting over time.

## Misc

**Q: How is Perfect Day different from Groupon or Eventbrite?**
A: Groupon focuses on broad deal listings without personalized daily planning. Eventbrite is a ticketing and event management tool for organizers. Perfect Day combines AI-personalized planning with integrated deals in a single flow — it is built around the user's day, not around generic discounts or ticket sales.

**Q: Can I plan events for others, not just myself?**
A: Yes. You can build and share event plans with others, assign roles, and collaborate on shared itineraries.

**Q: What if my plans change last minute?**
A: Perfect Day's dynamic scheduling feature automatically recalibrates your itinerary and updates offers when your plans shift.

**Q: Is there a community aspect to the app?**
A: Yes. A community platform for users to share ideas and stories is planned for a later sprint. Perfect Day also organizes community events, workshops, and activities to foster connection beyond the app.
